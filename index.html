📐 The NutriIQ Index

    
NutriIQ is a composite score (0–100) that measures how well a food serves your body, not just its macros. Designed by Phab's nutritionist team, it weights six dimensions.


    

      

        
⚡ Protein Efficiency 30%

        
Protein grams per 100 kcal. The single most predictive metric for satiety and muscle protein synthesis.

      

      

        
🩸 Glycaemic Load 20%

        
How sharply this food spikes blood sugar. Penalises high-GI carbs relative to fibre content.

      

      

        
🧂 Additive Penalty 20%

        
Deductions for artificial sweeteners, preservatives, emulsifiers, and added sugar above 5g/100g.

      

      

        
🥑 Fat Quality 15%

        
Ratio of unsaturated to saturated fat, with trans fat triggering a hard penalty.

      

      

        
🌾 Fibre Bonus 10%

        
Rewards dietary fibre ≥5g per serving, critical for gut health and sustained energy.

      

      

        
📦 Ingredient Transparency 5%

        
Shorter ingredient lists with recognisable names score higher. Rewards clean labels.
